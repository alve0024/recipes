Ingredients

1 head romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges 
1/2 each of a medium English cucumber and red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup pitted black olives
1 pkg. (200 g) Cracker Barrel Crumbled Feta Cheese with Oregano, Sun Dried Tomatoes and Black Pepper, crumbled
1/2 cup Kraft Classic Herb Dressing

How to prepare

Toss all ingredients except dressing in large salad bowl.
Add dressing just before serving; mix lightly.
